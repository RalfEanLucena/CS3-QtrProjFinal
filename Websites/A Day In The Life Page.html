Golden Retriever puppies are a common favorite for families. They are adorable and friendly as pups and can grow up to be just as happy and loving. The key to turning that cute little ball of fur into the perfect family pet is knowing which breed will best fit into your lifestyle. For a Golden Retriever, this means early and intensive training, a chance for lots of play, and a loving family to curl up with.

This breed is hardworking, intelligent, friendly and playful, which can all add up to the perfect family dog. There is a very good reason that Golden Retrievers are a popular choice for rescue, therapy, and service dogs.

This breed is hardworking and intelligent, meaning that they can enjoy their training and retain it very well. Since they are an active breed of dog, they enjoy working as much as they enjoy playing and will often seem to have a wide grin on their faces. These dogs can be a great comfort to victims of tragedy and will often serve as therapy dogs, patiently allowing themselves to be hugged and providing a comforting shoulder to cry on. For families, this can mean that a retriever is the perfect breed to be a friend and companion as well as a pet. If you are looking for a guard dog, then Golden Retriever puppies are not the ones for you. Chances are that this dog breed will lick an intruder to death instead of intimidating them to leave. On the plus side, if you are looking for a friendly dog to play catch with you in the park, then this breed is perfect. Retrievers really live up to their name, as they were bred to help hunters retrieve water fowl, and love to chase anything you throw for them.

Finding the right dog for your family is about more than just choosing a breed that works well with your lifestyle, but that is the best place to start. If you would like an active breed that loves to play and be cuddled, then you will want to look at various Golden Retriever puppies to find your new best friend.
